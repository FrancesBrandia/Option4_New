Access to Dev View to download Logos

To access Dev Mode and download logos as SVG, PNG, or JPG files, please go to your the “Examples” tab which will open the Figma file and toggle to Dev Mode using the Shift + D shortcut or the toggle in the toolbar. From there, you can easily inspect the designs and download any assets you need. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out!
